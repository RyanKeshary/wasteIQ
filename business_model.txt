# WASTE-IQ BUSINESS MODEL & REVENUE STRATEGY

WasteIQ is a Production-Grade Civic Intelligence Platform (CIP) designed to optimize urban sanitation through Real-Time OCR, IoT Telemetry, and High-Fidelity Communication.

## 1. TARGET SEGMENTS
- Municipal Corporations (B2G)
- Private Waste Management Contractors (B2B)
- Gated Communities & Large Townships (B2B)

## 2. REVENUE STREAMS

### A. SaaS SUBSCRIPTION (CORE)
- **Tiers**: Basic (Dashboard only), Pro (Real-time tracking), Enterprise (AI-OCR & Predictive Logistics).
- **Pricing**: Monthly fee based on the number of active collection units (trucks) or municipal wards covered.

### B. SMART HARDWARE INTEGRATION
- **IoT Sensors**: Recurring revenue from smart bin sensor monitoring (Ultrasonic fill sensors).
- **Hardware Markup**: One-time profit from selling WasteIQ-certified GPS and fill-level sensors.

### C. PREMIUM CITIZEN SERVICES (ON-DEMAND)
- **Priority Pickup**: Citizens pay a small convenience fee for guaranteed 2-hour pickup of oversized waste (furniture, debris) via the "SOS" feature.
- **Verification-as-a-Service**: Providing certified recycling data for corporations to meet their ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) goals.

### D. DATA INTELLIGENCE & ANALYTICS
- **Waste Composition Trends**: Selling anonymized data to FMCG brands about consumption patterns in specific wards.
- **Logistics Optimization**: Selling route-efficiency algorithms to private logistics firms.

### E. SECONDARY MARKETPLACE
- **Recycling Lead Gen**: Taking a commission on leads sent to scrap dealers and industrial recyclers when citizens report "Recyclable" waste.

## 3. COST STRUCTURE
- **Infrastructure**: AWS/Vercel hosting, PostgreSQL storage, Redis caching.
- **AI/OCR Cost**: Tesseract.js is open-source (zero cost per scan), but heavy analysis runs on serverless compute.
- **Communication**: Ably WebSocket and SMS gateway costs.

## 4. UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION (USP)
- **Hyper-Local Focus**: Multi-lingual support (Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati) ensures 100% ground-level penetration.
- **AI-Powered Validation**: OCR prevents fake collection reports, ensuring tax-payer money is spent efficiently.
- **Citizen Engagement**: Gamified "Green Score" increases civic participation, reducing the burden on municipal staff.
